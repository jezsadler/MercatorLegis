Necromunda Special Rules Quick Reference Generator

by RemySparks

This web tool aims to make Necromunda slightly more playable by collecting together the various rules needed for a specific gang and presenting them on a few pages, rather than scattered over several volumes.

It uses information about your gang from YakTribe, together with rules text compiled by Necrodamus.

Rules currently supported:

  • Skills
  • Gang-Specific Skills, including Orlock Legendary Names
  • Weapon Traits
  • Armour and Field Armour, including CGC masks
  • Goliath Genesmithing
  • Vehicle Locomotion, Upgrades, and Wargear
  • Wyrd Powers, including Delaque Psychoterica

Currently not supported:

  • Other Wargear
  • Van Saar Cyberteknika and Escher Chem-Alchemy
  • Nice formatting for actions granted by wargear/other rules.
  • Other special rules

Please let me know if you have any issues. You can contact me through my YakTribe profile (RemySparks) or via a Necromunda community group.
