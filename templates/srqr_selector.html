This web tool aims to make Necromunda slightly more playable by collecting together the various rules needed for a specific gang and presenting them on a few pages, rather than scattered over several volumes.

It uses information about your gang from YakTribe, together with rules text compiled by Necrovox.

Rules currently supported:

  • Skills
  • Gang-Specific Skills, including Orlock Legendary Names
  • Wyrd Powers, including Delaque Psychoterica
  • Goliath Genesmithing
  • Van Saar Cyberteknika
  • Chems, including Escher Chem-Alchemy
  • Weapon Traits
  • Armour and Field Armour, including CGC masks
  • Vehicle Locomotion, Upgrades, and Wargear
  • Nice formatting for actions granted by wargear/other rules.
  • Chains of rules granted by other rules.
  • Pet special rules.
  • Most Conditions applied by other keywords.
  • Most Hangers-On.

Currently not supported:

  • Other gang-specific Wargear
  • Other gang-specific special rules

Intentionally not included:

  • Rules that grant stat boosts that are automatically incorporated into fighter profiles.
  • Rules that impact events outside a game, e.g. gang selection, scenario selection, fighter advancement, trading post...
  • Details of rules that split into multiple options, where YakTribe doesn't display the selected option, e.g. Archaeotech Device, Cawdor Path of Faith.
  • Pet ownership rules.
  • Hangers-On whose rules don't have a keyword.
  • Dramatis Personae special rules.
